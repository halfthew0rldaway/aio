3. DESIGN SYSTEM — STRICT
Style: Neobrutalism

Characteristics:

Strong borders (2–4px solid black)

Hard shadows (offset, no blur)

Sharp edges or controlled radius (max rounded-xl)

Bold typography

High contrast

Solid color blocks

No transparency blur

No glassmorphism

No glow

No gradient spam

Fonts

Use proper fonts that fit neobrutalism:

Headings: Space Grotesk / Satoshi / General Sans

Body: Inter

Fallback system fonts

Implement correct font hierarchy:

Clear scale

Consistent spacing
